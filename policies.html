Policies

To rent a car from Washington Rent-A-Car you will need the following:

  • To be 25 years old (if you’re under 25, you may be required to pay a young driver’s fee)
  • A valid driver's license with a photo
  • A photo ID (For example: a driver’s license or passport)
  • Credit Card, Debit Card or Cash payment

Thinks to consider befor renting the car

After the reservation is finished you should come to our branch and pick up your rental, you sign off an agreement that in case of damage of the car (Use of the car on racing track) you pay for everything (Damages and the consumables if it has been on a racing track). The car should not exceed 1500km on one day and if you exceed it you pay a fee or sign a different contract. The contract starts at the moment you have reserved and lasts a minimum of 24 hours. The car should be returned at the place of collection. A 30 minute margin of lateness is acceptable but everything exceeding that will be considered a late delivery and will have a fee.
